App Name: Linkbase

Team: Robert Hernandez, Michael Leung, Barbara Ristau

Linkbase provides an engaging way for jobseekers and companies to interact. Companies provide a list of questions that jobseekers can respond to. And job seekers can also search Crunchbase’s API to find companies that have recently raised money and may therefore be hiring.

App Name: SnapMap

Team: Edison Lam, Jayson Wong, Lum Situ

SnapMap is an app that allows a user to post an image as a mean to showcase places, like a virtual tour seen through the view of a best friend. Share posts, and show others how cool places you care about are.

App Name: Roomy

Team: Ryan Liszewski, Dustyn August, Poojan Dave, Rodrigo Bell

Roomy is an app that creates a smart home for roommates, building an mobile ecosystem allowing everyone in a home to become more productive, social, and intelligent, together.

Living in a city like San Francisco is impossible without roommates. But living with roommates can be impossible. To ease the pain, we created an ecosystem to coordinate with your roommates. An app that lets you:

  • Know whether or not your roommates are at home.
  • Communicate with your roommates through the built-in group chat.
  • Create a to-do list to keep up with the chores, the bills, and any other tasks.
  • Includes a button to provide quick directions back home.

App Name: MessengerB

Team: Becky Chan

MessengerB allows users to connect with their friends and families via text messages for free! The users have the option to create their own account or log in via facebook.

What distinguishes MessengerB compared to other messaging apps in the market is that it has the functionality to determine the location of the user and their friends. More importantly, it provides users with the ability to see the local time of their friends and schedule when he/she would like the message to be delivered.

This app is ideal for communicating with friends and family who lives in other parts of the world or travels a lot.

App Name: Eventopia

Team: Thomas Zhu, Mitchell Wong

Eventopia is an app that brings awareness to all events happening in City College of San Francisco. This college specific app enables all events to be visible to all students. Students can create events, chat with other users, and post pictures/videos.
