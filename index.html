App Name: SF Art Openings

Team: Christopher Soria, Juan Hernandez, Yousef Kazerooni

Have you ever yearned for a stimulating experience after a long day at work? There are many websites offering you listings of events in the city, showing you rows after rows of events. But it is overwhelming to go through them in search of the best experience. SF Art Openings is our solution for you: Because what can be more engaging than an art opening, where you can connect with interesting people in a creative setting, have drinks, and an amazing night with the artist as your host!

SF Art Openings gives users the convenience of finding art receptions in the city and get to them within just a few taps. Want more information before you decide? You can find detailed information about an event and use the map feature to get there, or choose from one of your favorite car share services. Once at the gallery, SF Art Opening allows you to jump into the conversation right off the bat, with a bulletin board-like chat function. Start connecting with people and have a splendid evening with SF Art Openings!

App Name: Foto

Team: Jasmine Farrell, Manthan “Monte” Thakkar, Veronika Kotckovich

Foto allows users to save memorable and eventful moments as animated videos. These videos can be streamed onto several social sites including Twitter, Facebook, and Google+. The video feature makes sharing these moments among family and friends engaging, exciting, and entertaining, especially with our new emojis that you can drag around, scale and rotate as you like. Once you’re done you can paste them onto your memorable pictures.

Our app uses several third party libraries to achieve its awesome design:

  • Parse
  • Onboard (a framework for beautiful onboard experiences)
  • RESideMenu (a style side menu with parallax effect inspired by Dribbble shots)
  • RAReorderableLayout (a UICollectionView photo layout with an interactive drag-and-drop)
  • UIColor-Hex-Swift (create autoreleased color using a RGBA hex string)
  • AVFoundation (customizable camera effects)

App Name: Vector

Team: David Wayman, Eric Chu, Eric Zimmerman

Need to try a new food spot, bar or café? Vector is a simple, intuitive app that allows you and a friend to find a meeting spot exactly at the midpoint between your locations. Wherever the midpoint is calculated, icons pop up on a map near the midpoint, allowing you to choose the type of meeting place, such as a bar, café, bakery, restaurant, or supermarket. Use the convenience of meeting your friend somewhere in the middle with Vector.
